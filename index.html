Privacy Policy — Hadiyissa Dictionary

Effective: June 14, 2026  ·  Operated by Adiel Labs  ·  bogalehabtama@gmail.com

Data We Collect

Hadiyissa Dictionary does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no sign-up, and no servers.

  • Search queries are processed entirely on your device and are never sent anywhere.
  • Favorites are saved in a local database on your device only. Adiel Labs has no access to them.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Ad interaction events

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Adiel Labs. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

On iOS, you will be asked for permission before your advertising identifier is used for personalized ads. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

On Android, you can opt out via Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. If you believe your child has been affected in any way, contact us at bogalehabtama@gmail.com.
